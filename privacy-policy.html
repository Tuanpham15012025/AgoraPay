Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

AgoraPay (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Pi Network public username and ID (via Pi SDK authentication)
  • Transaction-related data (sender, recipient, Pi amount)
  • Optional contact info if provided (email, Telegram, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity via Pi Network
  • To enable peer-to-peer Pi payments
  • To prevent fraud and secure transactions

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is only shared with Pi Network for authentication and payment verification.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure server infrastructure to protect your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective Date”.
